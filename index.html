Most recreational BJJ practitioners keep grinding through the pain until one injury finally forces them off the mats. Then another. Then they "take a break" that turns into forever.

The real problem

It's not that you're not tough enough.

BJJ is hard on the body by design. If you train without a physical preparation strategy built specifically for grapplers, it's only a matter of time before the accumulation catches up with you.

The practitioners who roll pain-free into their 40s and 50s aren't tougher — they train smarter. They support their BJJ with targeted strength work, smart conditioning, and proper load management.
