Before you decide

The Blueprint is built with joint longevity as the primary design constraint, not performance output. Every exercise selection prioritises load tolerance and tissue resilience over intensity. If you have a specific issue, the Custom Coaching plan lets us work around it directly from week one — no generic modifications, just a program built for your actual situation.

No barbell required. The program is designed around equipment most people already have access to — dumbbells, resistance bands, and bodyweight movements. If you train at a fully-equipped gym, there are progressions for that too. Either way works.

That's exactly who this is built for. The program caps at 2 strength sessions per week, never scheduled on your BJJ or open mat days. Sessions run 45 minutes max. The entire premise is that BJJ comes first — always. If you train 5 days on the mats, there are still 2 days to build the physical base that supports it.

The Blueprint is a well-structured program you follow independently — it works for most recreational grapplers without modification. Custom Coaching starts from the same foundation, but adapts every variable to your specific context: injury history, schedule, training age, and goals. You also get weekly check-ins to course-correct in real time. If you're managing a chronic issue or want a coach in your corner, that's when Custom Coaching pays for itself.

Both plans include a free Review Session at the end of week 8. We go over what changed, what's still a work in progress, and map out what comes next — whether that's a follow-on program phase, continued coaching, or just a clear maintenance plan. You won't be left with a document and no direction.
